Natillas

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 (2-inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 1 (1 Â½-inch) piece lemon zest
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Steps

  1. Combine milk, cinnamon stick, and lemon zest in a saucepan over low heat. Cook for 10 minutes without letting it boil.

  2. Meanwhile, beat egg yolks and sugar in a bowl with an electric mixer until light and creamy. Beat in cornstarch. Continue beating until mixture is smooth and lump-free.

  3. Remove and discard cinnamon stick and lemon zest from milk. Gradually add egg yolk mixture to the milk, and beat with a whisk over low heat until mixture starts to thickens, about 10 minutes. Bring custard to a boil and immediately remove from heat.

  4. Pour custard into individual serving dishes and chill in the fridge until set, about 2 hours.
